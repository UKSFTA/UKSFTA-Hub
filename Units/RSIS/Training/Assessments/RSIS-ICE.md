### **SECTION 1: SIGNALS INTELLIGENCE (SIGINT)**

**1.1 [DIRECTION FINDING]**
You are monitoring a frequency and detect a strong signal spike (-30dBm) that fades rapidly as you move North.
*   What does this tell you about the transmitter's location relative to your movement?
*   How would you confirm the location using a second team (Triangulation)?

**1.2 [TRAFFIC ANALYSIS]**
You intercept a brief transmission: *"Package is moving. ETA Green-House in 5 mikes."*
*   Break down this intercept into: Who, What, Where, When.
*   What is your immediate advice to the Ground Commander?

### **SECTION 2: HUMAN INTELLIGENCE (HUMINT)**

**2.1 [TACTICAL QUESTIONING]**
You have detained a military-aged male (MAM) leaving a known IED factory. He is uncooperative.
*   What is your opening approach (ACE Interaction)?
*   Under the Laws of Armed Conflict (LOAC), what *cannot* you do to extract information?

**2.2 [SOURCE PROTECTION]**
A local civilian offers you the location of a weapons cache but demands anonymity.
*   How do you conduct the meet in a public market without compromising them?
*   What "Pocket Litter" or evidence do you look for to verify their reliability?

### **SECTION 3: SENSITIVE SITE EXPLOITATION (SSE)**

2.  A stack of paper maps with markings.
3.  A pile of mobile phones (SIM cards removed).
4.  A wall full of photos of local politicians.

You recover a hard drive.

*   Why is it critical to tag this item with the Grid Reference and Time Found?
