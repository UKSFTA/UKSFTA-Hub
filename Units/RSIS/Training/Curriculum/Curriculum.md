# [RESTRICTED] RSIS TRAINING CURRICULUM (THE GREY COURSE)

This document outlines the formalized syllabus for the RSIS Tactical Intelligence & Exploitation (TIE) qualification. Training is conducted in **2-hour modular sessions**.

---

## 1. PHASE 1: SELECTION & APTITUDE
**Objective:** Evaluation of candidate analytical reasoning and communication skills.

1. **Theoretical Review:** 30-minute Initial Candidate Evaluation (ICE) followed by a 90-minute practical data analysis exercise.
2. **Pattern Analysis:** 2-hour practical assessment identifying insurgent nodes from disparate data sets.
3. **Information Management:** 2-hour stress-test managing multiple intelligence feeds during active combat.

---

## 2. PHASE 2: CORE TECHNICAL SKILLS
**Objective:** Technical mastery of the Intelligence and Exploitation toolkit.

### 2.1 SIGNALS INTELLIGENCE (SIGINT) (2-HOUR MODULE)
- **Spectrum Monitoring:** Scanning the VHF/UHF spectrum via ACRE-2 systems.
- **Direction Finding (DF):** Establishing vectors to hostile transmitters using RSSI data.
- **Electronic Warfare (EW):** Protocol for jamming enemy communications and implementing emission control (EMCON).

### 2.2 SENSITIVE SITE EXPLOITATION (SSE) (2-HOUR MODULE)
- **Site Clearance:** Systematic forensic sweep of an objective.
- **Intel Triage:** Rapid identification of priority documents and hardware under time constraints.
- **Biometric Processing:** Utilization of ACE-3 biometrics for HVT verification.

### 2.3 TACTICAL QUESTIONING (TQ) (2-HOUR MODULE)
- **Engagement Protocols:** Methods of questioning (Direct vs. Indirect).
- **Legal Framework:** Adherence to unit ROE and international humanitarian law.
- **Reporting:** Conversion of raw field data into standardized Intelligence Reports (INTREP).

---

## 3. PHASE 3: SPECIALIST TRACKS
**Objective:** Refinement of skills for specific operational roles.

### TRACK A: TECHNICAL SPECIALIST (ACTIVE TARGETING) (2-HOUR MODULE)
- **Dynamic Targeting:** Real-time identification and tracking of HVTs.
- **ISR Overwatch:** Provision of tactical situational awareness via UAV assets.
- **Threat Warning:** Immediate dissemination of intercept-based threat warnings to ground forces.

### TRACK B: EXPLOITATION OPERATOR (FORWARD EXPLOITATION TEAM - FET) (2-HOUR MODULE)
- **Assault Integration:** Tactical movement in conjunction with SAS/SBS assault elements.
- **Rapid Forensics:** Triage of digital media and documents in a kinetic environment.
- **Field Interrogation:** Real-time questioning of detainees to generate immediate follow-on objectives.

---

## 4. QUALIFICATION
Successful completion of the Final Training Exercise (FTX) (2-hour duration) authorizes the candidate to wear the **RSIS Flash** and be attached to an operational TIE Cell.
