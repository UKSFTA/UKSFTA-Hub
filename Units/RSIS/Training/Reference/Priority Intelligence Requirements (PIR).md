# RSIS PIR Standards

Priority Intelligence Requirements for RSIS TIE Cells.

1. **Signals Environment:** Active frequencies, encryption types, and transmitter locations.
2. **Network Mapping:** Identifying HVT nodes and courier routes.
3. **Forensic High-Value:** Location of hard drives, codebooks, and pocket litter.
4. **HUMINT Potential:** Identifying potential informants or key personnel for interrogation.
