# SBS PIR Standards

Priority Intelligence Requirements for SBS maritime reccenaissance.

1. **Beachhead Data:** Gradient, obstacles, and soil consistency for landing.
2. **Vessel Identification:** Class, armament, and crew complement.
3. **Coastal Defences:** Radar sites, shore batteries, and patrol boat routes.
4. **Harbor Infrastructure:** Berth locations, fuel depots, and C2 nodes.
