# [RESTRICTED] SAS TECHNICAL STANDARDS: SOPs & EQUIPMENT

This document defines the formalized standard operating procedures (SOPs) for SAS tactical equipment and systems.

---

## 1. COMMUNICATIONS (ACRE-2)
- **Signal Discipline:** 100mW - 1W for intra-team; 5W+ for HQ/Long-range links.
- **Brevity:** Adherence to standardized UKSF brevity codes.
- **Authentication:** Mandatory utilization of Daily Authentication Tables for all transmissions.

---

## 2. ADVANCED DEMOLITIONS (ACE-3)
- **Individual Loadout:** Standard issue of 1x M112 block and 1x Clacker per trooper.
- **Breaching Protocols:** Standardized placement for linear and frame charges on reinforced structures.

---

## 3. NAVIGATION & cTAB
- **BFT Management:** Synchronization of troop movement and data via the rugged tablet.
- **Data Entry:** Standardized icons for LUP (Lying Up Point), ERV (Emergency Rendezvous), and operational Objectives.
