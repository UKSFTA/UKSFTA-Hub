# SAS PIR Standards

Priority Intelligence Requirements for SAS reccenaissance patrols.

1. **Hostile Composition:** Total strength, weapons, and uniforms.
2. **Key Terrain:** High ground, water sources, and cover/concealment routes.
3. **Logistics:** Supply routes, vehicle types, and fuel storage.
4. **C2 Nodes:** Radio masts, command tents, and HVT locations.
