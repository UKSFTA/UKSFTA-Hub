# [RESTRICTED] SBS TECHNICAL STANDARDS: MARITIME SOPs

This document defines the formalized standard operating procedures (SOPs) for SBS specialized maritime equipment and systems.

-

## 1. MARITIME COMMUNICATIONS
- **Waterproofing:** Protocols for the protection of communication equipment during amphibious infiltration.
- **SATCOM:** Employment of maritime satellite communications for long-range data transmission.

-

## 2. COMBAT DIVING (ACE-3)
- **Rebreather SOP:** Management of oxygen levels and depth/time limits.
- **Sub-Surface Navigation:** Utilization of dive boards and specialized underwater navigation tools.

-

## 3. SURFACE CRAFT OPERATIONS
- **Stealth Protocols:** Speed and noise discipline during coastal approaches (RHIB/Klepper).
- **Infiltration Hardware:** Tactical employment of ladders, grapnel systems, and fast-rope gear.
