# [RESTRICTED] RSIS FORM 02-FSA: FIELD SKILLS ASSESSMENT

**UNIT:** RSIS - Training Wing
**LOCATION:** Simulated FOB / MOUT Facility
**DURATION:** 2 Hours

---

### **OBJECTIVE**
Verify the candidate's ability to act as the "Technical Edge" in a kinetic environment.

---

### **PHASE 1: THE TECHNICAL LISTENER (SIGINT)**
*Scenario: Candidate is placed in a listening post (LP) with an ACRE radio rack and a Spectrum Analyser.*

**Tasks:**
1.  **Scan:** Identify the active OPFOR frequency within a 10MHz range.
2.  **Intercept:** Successfully record 3 distinct enemy reports (Location, Strength, Intent).
3.  **Locate:** Direct a friendly patrol to the enemy transmitter using signal strength readings only.

**Grading:**
*   [ ] Frequency identified < 2 mins.
*   [ ] Accuracy of intercept logs.
*   [ ] Vectoring accuracy (friendly patrol < 50m from target).

---

### **PHASE 2: THE CRIME SCENE (SSE)**
*Scenario: A room has been "cleared" by SF. Candidate has 3 minutes to exploit.*

**Tasks:**
1.  **Secure:** Identify and bag all "Intel Items" (Phones, Laptops, Documents).
2.  **Filter:** Ignore "Decoy" items (Empty wrappers, irrelevant books).
3.  **Analyse:** Find the hidden link (e.g., a phone number on a napkin matches a previous HVT).

**Grading:**
*   [ ] % of valid intel recovered.
*   [ ] Zero "Decoy" items taken (Weight discipline).
*   [ ] Identification of the "Golden Nugget" (Key intel piece).

---

### **PHASE 3: THE HUMAN ELEMENT (TQ)**
*Scenario: Interaction with a roleplayer (Civilian or EPW).*

**Tasks:**
1.  **Control:** Establish dominance/rapport without excessive force.
2.  **Question:** Use "Direct" vs "Screening" questions to elicit the HVT's location.
3.  **Search:** Perform a thorough ACE prisoner search for hidden weapons or intel.

**Grading:**
*   [ ] Safety (Candidate did not get stabbed/shot).
*   [ ] Intel gained (Location revealed).
*   [ ] Adherence to LOAC.
