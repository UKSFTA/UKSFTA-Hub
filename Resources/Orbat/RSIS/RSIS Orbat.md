**Role Definitions and Primary Duties**

- **Intelligence Discipline Lead:** Oversees the Intelligence Collection Plan (ICP). Coordinates directly with the SRR for surveillance requirements and advises the E-Squadron Ground Commander on "High-Value Target" (HVT) locations and patterns of life.
    
- **Human Intelligence (HUMINT) Operator:** Specialized in Tactical Questioning (TQ) and Source Handling. In a realistic Arma scenario, they manage civilian interactions (CIMIC) and conduct the formal screening of detainees (EPWs) to extract time-sensitive tactical data.
    
- **Signals Intelligence (SIGINT) / EW Operator:** Responsible for Electronic Warfare and monitoring the electromagnetic spectrum. They utilize Direction Finding (DF) to locate enemy radio emitters and provide early warning of enemy QRF activation via intercepted communications.
    
- **Exploitation & Forensic Specialist:** Often referred to as a **Weapons Intelligence Technician (WIT)**. They conduct **Sensitive Site Exploitation (SSE)**. Their role is to process "Pocket Litter," digital media (DOMEX), and biometric data from a target site to identify insurgent networks and IED makers.
    
- **All-Source Analyst / G2 Liaison:** The "Fusion" point. They take the raw drone feeds (IMINT), the radio intercepts (SIGINT), and the scout reports (SRR) to build the Common Operating Picture (COP). They maintain the "Enemy Situation Map" (SITMAP) in real-time.
