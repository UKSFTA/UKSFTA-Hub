# UK Military Ranks

This note provides a reference for the rank structure of the British Army, which is the primary rank structure used in the UK Special Forces.

## Officer Ranks
- Field Marshal (OF-10)
- General (OF-9)
- Lieutenant General (OF-8)
- Major General (OF-7)
- Brigadier (OF-6)
- Colonel (OF-5)
- Lieutenant Colonel (OF-4)
- Major (OF-3)
- Captain (OF-2)
- Lieutenant (OF-1)
- Second Lieutenant (OF-1)
- Officer Cadet (OF-D)

## Other Ranks (Enlisted)
- Warrant Officer Class 1 (WO1)
- Warrant Officer Class 2 (WO2)
- Staff Sergeant (SSgt)
- Sergeant (Sgt)
- Corporal (Cpl)
- Lance Corporal (LCpl)
- Private (Pte)
